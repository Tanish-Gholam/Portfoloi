National Budget at Collegiate Level

Presentation on Ministry of Agriculture – Chetana College

During the National Budget Presentation at Chetana College in December 2023, I represented the Ministry of Agriculture. The session aimed at simulating a real-world government budget discussion, encouraging critical thinking and financial awareness among students.

Key Points Covered:

  • Overview of the current agricultural sector in India
  • Allocation of funds towards rural infrastructure and irrigation
  • Support for organic and sustainable farming
  • Technological integration in farming – AI and IoT initiatives
  • Farmer insurance schemes and subsidy management

I emphasized the government's commitment to doubling farmers’ income and reducing agricultural dependency on monsoon. The presentation included a statistical breakdown of past expenditures and the proposed budget for the coming fiscal year.

It was a proud moment to simulate real parliamentary behavior, with question-answer sessions, inter-ministry debates, and active participation from fellow students.
